How to make a sandwich

  1. Get your favourite bread

    2. Sliced white, brown or if you're feeling fancy, a ciabatta

  2. Sauce it up

    3. Nobody likes a dry sandwich - get some mayo, mustard, butter, or all 3 and smear both sides of the bread

  3. Add some fillings

    4. Anything goes really here, but don't go OTT - too much of a good thing applies

  4. Add some salad

    5. Essential IMO, unless you're making a toastie!

  5. Time to build

    6. Place bread on top of the fillings, slice in half - horizontal or diagonal? Up to you
